# Bangladeshi Monopoly - Game Rules

## üéØ Objective

Be the last player remaining with money and properties. Bankrupt all other players to win!

## üéÆ Game Setup

### Starting the Game
1. **Create or Join a Room** - One player creates a room and shares the 6-digit code
2. **Wait for Players** - 2-8 players can join
3. **Ready Up** - All players click "Ready"
4. **Start** - The host starts the game

### Initial Setup
- Each player starts with **‡ß≥15,000**
- All players begin at **GO**
- Turn order is randomized
- All properties are unowned

## üé≤ Turn Structure

### 1. Roll Dice
- Click "Roll Dice" on your turn
- Move your token clockwise around the board
- The sum of both dice determines spaces moved

### 2. Landing Actions
Depending on where you land:

#### **Unowned Property**
- Option to **Buy** at list price
- If you decline, property goes to **Auction** (all players can bid)

#### **Owned Property (Not Yours)**
- Pay **rent** to the owner
- Rent amount depends on:
  - Base rent (no buildings)
  - Houses built (1-4)
  - Hotel built (worth 5 houses)
  - Monopoly status (double rent if no buildings)

#### **Your Own Property**
- Nothing happens
- Safe space!

#### **Special Spaces**
- **GO**: Collect ‡ß≥2000 salary
- **Chance**: Draw a Chance card
- **Community Chest**: Draw a Community Chest card
- **Income Tax**: Pay ‡ß≥2000
- **Luxury Tax**: Pay ‡ß≥1000
- **Free Parking**: Just rest (no action)
- **Go to Jail**: Move directly to Jail
- **Just Visiting**: No penalty if just passing through Jail

### 3. End Turn
- After all actions, click "End Turn"
- Turn passes to the next player

## üè† Building System

### Requirements to Build
1. **Own ALL properties in a color group** (Monopoly)
2. **No mortgaged properties** in that group
3. **Have enough money** for building cost
4. **Even building rule**: Must build evenly across the color group
   - Can't have 2 houses on one property and 0 on another in same group

### Building Types
- **Houses**: Build 1-4 houses on each property
  - Cost varies by property (shown on property card)
  - Rent increases with each house
- **Hotels**: Requires 4 houses first
  - Exchange 4 houses for 1 hotel
  - Highest rent level
  - Returns houses to bank

### Building Inventory
- **32 Houses** total in the game
- **12 Hotels** total in the game
- If inventory is empty, you must wait for buildings to become available

### Selling Buildings
- Sell back to bank for **50% of building cost**
- Must sell evenly (same rule as building)
- Hotels must be downgraded to 4 houses before selling houses

## üí∞ Mortgage System

### Mortgaging Properties
- Receive **50% of property price**
- Mortgaged properties:
  - Cannot collect rent
  - Cannot build on
  - Still owned by you
- Must sell all buildings on a color group before mortgaging

### Unmortgaging Properties
- Pay **55% of property price** (50% + 10% interest)
- Property becomes active again
- Can now collect rent

## ü§ù Trading System

### How to Trade
1. Click "Trade" button
2. Select opponent player
3. Choose what you **Offer**:
   - Properties
   - Money
4. Choose what you **Request**:
   - Properties
   - Money
5. Send trade proposal

### Trade Rules
- Can only trade with one player at a time
- Cannot trade mortgaged properties with buildings
- Trades are "all or nothing" (atomic)
- Opponent can **Accept** or **Reject**
- No partial trades

### Trading Tips
- Use trades to complete monopolies
- Trade to get out of financial trouble
- Balance property values fairly

## üî® Auction System

### When Auctions Occur
- When a player lands on an unowned property and declines to buy

### Auction Rules
1. **All players can bid** (including the player who declined)
2. **30-second timer** per auction
3. **Minimum bid**: ‡ß≥10 above current bid
4. **Quick bid buttons**: +‡ß≥10, +‡ß≥50, +‡ß≥100, +‡ß≥500
5. Highest bidder wins when timer expires

### Auction Strategy
- Properties can sell below OR above list price
- Strategic properties (monopoly completion) often go higher
- Don't overbid if you can't afford it!

## üöî Jail System

### Going to Jail
Three ways to go to jail:
1. Land on "Go to Jail" space
2. Draw a "Go to Jail" card
3. Roll doubles 3 times in a row

### Getting Out of Jail
Three ways to get out:
1. **Roll doubles** on your turn (up to 3 attempts)
2. **Pay ‡ß≥500 fine** before rolling
3. **Use Get Out of Jail Free card** (from Chance/Community Chest)

### Jail Rules
- You're "Just Visiting" if you land on Jail normally
- While in jail:
  - Can still collect rent
  - Can still trade
  - Can still build
- After 3 failed rolls, MUST pay fine and move

## üí≥ Chance & Community Chest Cards

### Card Types
**Money Cards:**
- Collect money from bank
- Pay money to bank
- Collect from all players
- Pay all players

**Movement Cards:**
- Move to specific location
- Move to nearest utility/station
- Go back 3 spaces
- Go to Jail

**Special Cards:**
- Get Out of Jail Free (keep until used)
- Building repairs (pay per house/hotel)

### Card Rules
- Cards are shuffled at game start
- After drawing, card goes to bottom of deck
- Get Out of Jail Free cards can be traded!

## üí∏ Bankruptcy

### When You're Bankrupt
- Cannot pay rent, taxes, or other obligations
- Don't have enough assets to liquidate

### Liquidation Order
1. **Sell buildings** (at 50% value)
2. **Mortgage properties** (at 50% value)
3. If still can't pay ‚Üí **Declare Bankruptcy**

### Bankruptcy Effects
- All assets transfer to creditor (if bankrupt to a player)
- All assets return to bank (if bankrupt to bank)
- You're out of the game
- Last player standing wins!

## üèÜ Winning the Game

### Victory Conditions
**Primary:** Be the last player remaining (all others bankrupt)

**Alternative (House Rules):**
- Time limit reached - Richest player wins
- Total worth = Cash + Property values + Building values

## ‚ö° Special Rules & Tips

### Doubles Rule
- **Rolling doubles** (both dice same number):
  - Roll again after moving
  - Can roll up to 3 times
  - **3 doubles in a row** = Go to Jail

### Passing GO
- Collect ‡ß≥2000 salary every time you pass or land on GO
- Exception: "Go to Jail" card/space (no salary)

### Free Parking (Optional)
- Traditional Monopoly: Nothing happens
- House rule: Collect jackpot from taxes and fines

### Strategy Tips
1. **Early Game**: Buy properties aggressively
2. **Mid Game**: Focus on completing monopolies
3. **Late Game**: Build hotels on high-rent properties
4. **Always keep cash reserve** for rent payments
5. **Stations and utilities** provide steady income
6. **Orange properties** are most landed on (statistically)
7. **Don't overbuild** if it leaves you cash-poor

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I trade Get Out of Jail Free cards?**
A: Yes! They can be traded like properties.

**Q: What happens if I can't pay rent?**
A: You must liquidate assets (sell buildings, mortgage properties) until you can pay. If you still can't, you're bankrupt.

**Q: Can I build hotels without buying houses first?**
A: No. You must build 4 houses first, then upgrade to a hotel.

**Q: Do I have to buy property if I land on it?**
A: No, but if you decline, it goes to auction where someone else might buy it.

**Q: Can I collect rent while in Jail?**
A: Yes! You can collect rent, trade, and build while in Jail.

**Q: What happens if houses/hotels run out?**
A: You must wait for other players to sell buildings back to the bank.
